RECURRING DEPOSIT

Recurring deposits is the ideal alternative for people who systematically want financial planning. A small portion of their regular monthly income will be deposited with the rate of interest higher than a regular savings deposit. Premature withdrawal, Loans, and nomination services are available subject to conditions.

FIXED DEPOSIT

The ICCSL credit society accepts deposits for a minimum period of 1 year to a maximum of 7 years. The rate of interest that we provide for account holders are marginally higher than the regular savings deposit holders.

DAILY DEPOSIT

To enhance the savings habits of our members, we have introduced this money management account. Customers can save money and yield a small rate of interest. It is very convenient to withdraw and deposit in your account by just using your withdrawal slip.
