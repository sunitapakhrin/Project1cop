FUNCTIONING OF MEMBERSHIP ICCSL

To obtain membership of the ICCSL, you are expected to apply for at least one share of the Society bearing the face value of ₨100, and it is the discretion of management of the ICCSL whether to approve it or not. Once the membership is allotted, the respective member is given the right to take part in the activities of the management of the organization through the A. G. M. (Annual General Meeting) other than various other rights.

You are welcome to keep on registering your suggestions for improvement of the functioning of the society. ICCSL society functions in a perfectly well mode and our spirit of professionalism makes us treat our members as part of ICCSL family. Our cooperative efforts are targeted towards ensuring the welfare of our members through the medium of financial support. We also render technology-driven financial aid under one roof.

MEMBER OF THE SOCIETY

Any person, who has completed 18 years, is qualified for entering a valid contract. The society also demands that a person applying for membership here should not possess membership at any other Credit Co-operative Society, and moreover he/she should not be punished for any criminal offence by the court of law, and the applicant should be a resident of India.
