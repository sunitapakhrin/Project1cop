WHO WE ARE

RIA was founded when co-operation was just beginning to gain ground in the country. It was set up with the primary
objective of assisting the less fortunate members of the community in its economic enterprises.

A TRUE AND PROGRESSIVE CO-OPERATIVE CREDIT SOCIETY

Nepal Cooperative Credit Society Limited (NCCSL) has always worked for the progress of the community with an aim to completely dedicate our services for the welfare of the shareholders in Kathmandu, Dharan, Butwal, Biratnagar. We hold the distinction of being the sole credit co-operative society in Kathmandu and the aforesaid Nepal states with many schemes and financial plans for less fortunate peoples in communities and enterprises. However, it takes something more than special to make a credit co-operative society to bring a difference in people's lives and the society in a whole. It is exactly become an important factor since our inception. Today, we are a model credit co-operative society in Kathmandu with a track record with more than 300,000 happy members and 4000 advisors in Kathmandu.

RECURRING DEPOSIT

Recurring deposits is the ideal alternative for people who systematically want financial planning. A small portion of their regular monthly income will be deposited with the rate of interest higher than a regular savings deposit.Loans of different nomination services are available subject to conditions.

FIXED DEPOSIT

The RIA credit society accepts deposits for a minimum period of 1 year to a maximum of 7 years. The rate of interest that we provide for to you in your account holders are marginally higher than the regular savings deposit holders.

DAILY DEPOSIT

To enhance the savings habits of our members, we have introduced this money management account.To our Customers you can save money and yield a small rate of interest. For It is very convenient and easy to withdraw and deposit in your account by just using your withdrawal slip.

OUR SERVICE

RIA is completely autonomously operated association with an open membership for like-minded people. We offer banking services to shareholders who often have limited help from big banks. We offer our customers mostly from our local structured community with banking services and schemes which are of reasonable interest rates and are implemented with a great degree of personalization to people who require home loans, agriculture loans, and deposits schemes.
